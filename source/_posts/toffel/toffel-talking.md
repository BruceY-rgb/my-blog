### 2.2 论证方法

#### 2.2.1 举例论证

通过个人经历或观察来支撑观点，帮助回答更具真实性和说服力。

**结构：背景 → 过程 → 结果**

- **背景**：介绍时间、地点和人物等背景信息
- **过程**：解释你采取了哪些行动
- **结果**：总结这些行动带来的效果或变化（短期/长期影响 + 感悟）

!!! example "Describe your healthy diet"
- **背景**：For example, I've always tried to **maintain a balanced diet** to ensure I stay healthy. I focus on eating **a variety of whole foods**.
- **过程**：Every day, I start my day with a **nutritious breakfast** like **oatmeal with fruits or eggs** with **whole grain toast**. For lunch and dinner, I make sure to include vegetables, **lean proteins** like chicken or tofu, and healthy fats like **avocado or olive oil**.
- **结果**：As a result, I feel **more energized and rarely get sick**. I've noticed that my **skin has improved**, and I am able to **stay focused** and **productive** throughout the day.
!!!

!!! note "食物类语料积累"
- I try to eat a balanced diet **by including** a variety of fruits, vegetables, and proteins in my meals.
- I avoid processed foods because **they tend to be high in** sugar, salt and unhealthy fats.
- My diet **consists mainly of** whole foods like vegetables, whole grains and lean proteins.
- I make sure to drink **plenty of water** throughout the day to **stay hydrated**.
- I prefer **home-cooked meals** because I can **control the ingredients** and make them healthier.
- My goal is to eat foods that are rich in **vitamins and minerals** to **boost my immune system**.
!!!

#### 2.2.2 利弊分析法

将论据清晰划分为利弊两类，通过理性权衡让论证更客观、有说服力。

**结构：好处 → 潜在挑战/缺点**

- **好处**：描述该事物或观点的正面影响（个人、社会、工作等角度）
- **潜在问题**：反思其可能带来的负面影响，或在某些情况下的不适应性

!!! example "What kind of reaction do you have to social media?"
- **好处**
  - It helps me **stay in touch with my friends and family**, even those **who live far away**.
  - I can also follow **educational accounts** that help me **expand my knowledge**.
- **潜在问题**
  - Social media can be addictive. Spending too much time on it can lead to anxiety and **distract from real-life interactions**.
  - Moreover, there is also the risk of **privacy invasion**, as personal information is often **shared without full awareness of its consequences**.
!!!

#### 2.2.3 数据/案例佐证法

通过引用公开的数据、新闻案例、研究结果或常识性事实，支持和增强自己的观点。

**结构：引用数据/案例 → 解释分析 → 总结结论**

**引用数据/案例的常用表达**

- `according to...`
- `research shows that...`
- `statistics indicate that...`
- `a recent study found that...`
- `data from [source] reveals...`
- `surveys suggest that...`

**分析数据的常用表达**

- `This data suggests that...`
- `What we can infer from this data is that...`
- `This implies that...`
- `the findings show that...`
- `this highlights the importance of...`

**总结结论的常用表达**

- `This means that...` 这意味着
- `In other words` 换句话说
- `That is to say` 也就是说
- `What this shows is...` 这表明
- `This can be interpreted as...` 这可以解读为
- `As a result, we can see that...` 结果，我们可以看到

!!! example "环境保护类"
Going paperless isn't just convenient—it's also environmentally friendly. For example, **studies show that** **saving one ton of paper can protect about 17 trees and save 7000 gallons of water**. That's significant, because **each tree produces enough oxygen for 3 people per day**. So **if we do** paperless reading, it could save thousands of trees and gallons of water every day.
!!!

!!! example "Online learning"
- **现象**：The rise of online learning has become a significant trend in education, **especially during the pandemic**.
- **原因**：The main reason for the increase in online learning is **the advancement of technology and the necessity brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic**, which **forced schools and universities to adapt to remote learning methods**.
- **结果**：As a result, online learning has brought significant benefits such as **greater flexibility**, **allowing students to learn at their own pace, and providing access to a wider range of educational resources**.
!!!

**结构：明确现象 → 分析原因 → 分析结果 → 总结(解决方案)**

#### 2.2.5 对比法

尤其适用于偏好类、二选一题目。从多个角度分析问题，增强论证深度。

**结构：引入对比事物 → 对比分析（强调差异和优势）**

!!! example "Do you prefer reading physical books or e-books?"
**选 e-book**

Physical books are often **bulky and heavy**, which makes it difficult to carry a lot of them at once. **In contrast**, e-books are extremely convenient. You can carry an entire library in a single device, which makes them great for travelling or when you need **access to** multiple books at once. Another advantage of e-books is **customization**. I can easily adjust the font size, brightness and background color to suit my reading preference. This feature makes reading much more comfortable, especially in low-light environments. Moreover, e-books are **more environmentally friendly** because they do not require paper. **So I feel better about reducing my environmental footprint**.
!!!

#### 2.2.6 引入假设情景

通过假设未来情景来展开论述，适用于预测类题目。

!!! example "What is your reaction to robots becoming the mainstream development direction in the future"
- **引入假设情景**：If robots were to become the mainstream development in the future, we could **expect significant changes in many industries and aspects of daily life**.
- **推导正面影响**：**On the positive side**, robots could **bring about increased efficiency and productivity**. For example, in industries like manufacturing or healthcare, robots can perform repetitive or dangerous tasks more quickly and accurately than humans. **This would lead to lower costs and higher-quality products**.
- **推导负面影响**：**However, there could also be some negative consequences to** the rise of robots. One concern is that **job displacement** could occur, as robots might **replace human workers in many sectors**, leading to unemployment and economic inequality.
!!!

### 2.3 答题结构模板

#### 基础模板：P.A.S.S.

**P - Position 立场：直接表态**

- `I'd say my choice is...`
- `Personally, I prefer...`
- `I think... is better for me...`
- `For me, the best option is...`
- **`If I have to choose, I'd go with...`**

**A - Argument 论点：给出一到两个理由**

- **`The main reason is that...`**
- `This is mostly because...`
- `One clear advantage is that...`

**S - Support 论据：细节例子**

- `For example/instance, take... as an example`
- `such as/like/I mean/to be specific/especially...`

**S - Summary 总结：避免突然停住**

- `So this is why I feel this way`
- **`So that's basically my view on this`**
- `So that's why I would choose that option`

**First Reason**

- `First...` + `To be more specific...`

**Second Reason**

- `Second...` + `For example...`

#### 高级模板

- `Personally, I believe [main point]`
- `This is primarily because [reason]`
- `What I mean is [elaborate on reason]`
- `Not only that, but [reason 2]`
- `for instance, [elaborate on reason or give example]`

## 3. 题型范文库(运动健康类)

### 3.1 偏好类（二选一）

!!! example "Would you prefer to exercise alone or with others?"
I prefer **exercising with others** rather than alone.

Exercising with others provides **more motivation and support**, while exercising alone often relies solely on self-discipline. This difference is important because having companions encourages me to **push myself harder** and **keeps me consistent with my routine**.

**For instance**, when I go jogging with friends, their energy and encouragement motivate me to run faster and finish the full distance. **On the other hand**, when I run alone, it's easy to slow down or skip part of the workout, especially when I feel tired.

So that's why I would choose that option.
!!!

### 3.2 趋势预测类

**通用答题框架**

```
I believe ... will become a lot more popular in the future.
One main reason is that ... (趋势原因)
... bring many benefits: ... (好处)
For example, ... (例子)
As ..., ... will only become more common. (未来更普遍)
```

!!! example "室内健身房更受欢迎"
I believe indoor gyms will become more popular in the future.

One main reason is the development of fitness technology, which has greatly lowered the entry barrier. So gyms are more affordable, easier to join, and equipped with modern, user-friendly facilities.

The biggest advantages of indoor gyms are high efficiency and stability. Unlike outdoor activities, gyms are not affected by weather, time, or pollution. People can walk in anytime and use professional equipment immediately, allowing them to exercise in a focused way without wasting time.

For example, busy office workers can finish a complete workout in just 30 to 40 minutes, no matter if it's rainy, cold, or dark outside.

As people value time efficiency and convenient fitness more than ever, indoor gyms will definitely become more common.
!!!

!!! example "户外活动更受欢迎"
I think outdoor activities will become a lot more popular in the future.

I guess life is getting more stressful, and **many people are tired of being stuck indoors all day**. **That's why more and more people are looking for fun, natural ways to relax and unwind.**

The best part about being outside is that fresh air, sunlight, and open space really help people **calm down and recharge**. It just feels way more relaxing than staying in a crowded gym.

For example, many young people now choose hiking on weekends rather than going to gyms. **They enjoy nature and share their trips online, making this trend even more popular.**

**As people care more about mental well-being and healthy lifestyles, outdoor activities will only become more common**.
!!!

### 3.3 描述类

从 6 个维度回答：`what`, `who`, `when`, `where`, `how often`, `why`

**流程描述：Routine Cycle (3-step routine)**

```
warm-up → main workout → cool-down
```

!!! example "描述一个运动"
I usually play badminton with my roommate in the university sports center. We go there about twice a week, normally on Tuesday and Friday evenings, because it's close to our dorm and easy to book a court.

We always start with a quick warm-up, like light stretching and a few practice swings to loosen our shoulders. Then we move into the main part: a 30-minute rally where we practice footwork, smashes, and quick net shots. **That's the most intense part and it really gets my heart rate up.**

After that, **we cool down by slowing the pace and doing a short walk around the gym to relax our muscles.**
!!!

!!! example "个人成长/改变类模板"
**通用结构：过去问题 → 采取行动 → 过程挑战 → 积极改变 → 未来展望**

| 步骤 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 过去 | `I used to...` / `I often felt... and found it hard to...` |
| 改变 | `But later, I started to...` |
| 过程 | `During this process, I had to...` |
| 结果 | `Gradually, I became much more... and my... improved a lot.` |
| 展望 | `I believe this will bring me a huge competitive advantage in my future career.` |

**范文：团队运动 → 沟通能力提升**

I used to get extremely nervous when I talked to people. I often felt shy and struggled to express my ideas clearly.

But later, I started to take part in team sports and work closely with my teammates. During practices and games, I had to communicate with them constantly to share plans and give suggestions.

Gradually, I became much more confident and my communication skills improved significantly. Now I can talk to others comfortably and express myself fluently.

I believe strong communication skills will bring me a huge competitive advantage in my future career and help me stand out in the workplace.

**变体：小组项目 → 合作能力**

I used to get extremely nervous when I worked with others. I was shy and didn't know how to share my ideas or cooperate with teammates.

But later, I started to take part in group projects and team activities. I learned to listen to others, express my opinions politely and work with my partners to solve problems together.

Gradually, I became much more confident and my collaboration skills improved significantly.

I believe good collaboration skills will give me a strong competitive advantage in my future career and help me fit in well in the workplace.
!!!

### 3.4 同意反对类

!!! example "健身App有助于追踪目标"
Yes, I do think fitness apps are a great way to track exercise goals.

One big reason is you can set clear, specific goals. These apps can design a workout plan based on your own details like your weight, height, and fitness goals, so the plan feels much more tailored than just following a random video online. This gives you a clear direction.

Another advantage is real-time data. For example, the app records how long I exercised, how many calories I burned, even my heart rate and step count. When I see those numbers improving week by week, it gives me a real sense of progress and motivates me to keep going.

Finally, it's super convenient. Most apps provide weekly or monthly reports. By looking at the charts and statistics, you can easily see your improvements and adjust your plan if necessary.

Overall, fitness apps make exercise more structured, flexible, and motivating.
!!!

!!! example "户外活动对儿童健康至关重要"
I do think outdoor activities are essential for children's health, especially because they help build a stronger immune system. Children are still developing their immunity, and they actually need exposure to natural elements like sunlight, fresh air, and even small amounts of harmless bacteria to train their bodies to fight off illnesses. That's something indoor activities really can't offer.

For example, my cousin's daughter used to stay indoors often and got sick easily. Later, she started playing outside on weekends, running on grass and enjoying nature. After several months, she became much healthier and rarely caught colds.

So from this perspective, outdoor activities aren't just "fun." They provide natural conditions that support the immune system during a critical stage of childhood development.
!!!

!!! example "规律运动培养自律"
I agree that regular exercise is essential for developing self-discipline.

Self-discipline means sticking to a plan, staying consistent, and keeping promises to ourselves, even when we feel tired or unwilling.

Regular exercise truly trains these qualities. For instance, we have to get up early to work out instead of sleeping in, keep exercising even when our bodies feel sore, and follow a planned schedule rather than giving up halfway. This process forces us to put long-term health ahead of short-term comfort.

In this way, exercise helps us build strong self-discipline step by step.
!!!

## 4. 逻辑连接词库

#### 1. 观点引入 / 总起

用于开头明确立场或概括核心内容。

- `In my opinion...` / `From my perspective...`
- `As far as I'm concerned...`
- `Personally, I think...`
- `The main point is that...`

#### 2. 分点论述（并列 / 递进）

**并列关系**
- `First` / `First of all` / `To start with`
- `Second` / `Secondly` / `Then`
- `Third` / `Thirdly` / `Finally` / `Last but not least`
- `For one thing... (and) for another...`
- `And` / `Also` / `as well as` / `not only... but also...`

**递进关系**
- `Besides` / `In addition` / `Additionally`
- `Moreover` / `Furthermore` / `What's more` / `On top of that`

#### 3. 举例说明

- `For example` / `For instance`
- `One example is...`

### 5.2 介绍一本读过的书

**答题框架**

**45秒压缩版**

!!! example "冒险小说"
The last book I read was during a stressful period. I wanted something relaxing, so I read it before bed.

It's an adventure novel. The story follows characters who travel to an unknown place and face unexpected challenges. **What I enjoyed most was the fast-paced plot and the sense of exploration**. **The language is vivid, so it's easy to imagine the scenes**.

**Overall, the experience was exciting and immersive**. It helped me forget about stress. The only downside was some parts felt a bit unrealistic, but for an adventure story, that's acceptable.
!!!

!!! example "自助成长类书籍"

It's a non-fiction book focused on psychology and human behavior. The author explains why people develop certain habits and how they affect productivity. I liked that the author used real-life examples instead of heavy academic language.

**The reading experience was practical and motivating.** I often paused to think about how the ideas applied to my life. However, some parts felt repetitive.

### 5.4 阅读偏好对比

**提问**：Do you prefer fiction or non-fiction for pleasure reading?

**45秒压缩版**

!!! example "偏好虚构类"
For pleasure reading, I'd prefer fiction books.

The main reason is that fiction helps me relax more easily. Compared to non-fiction, **I don't need to focus on facts—I can just follow the story and enjoy it**.

Another reason is that fiction is more **emotionally engaging**. **I get involved in the characters and the plot, which makes reading feel like entertainment, not effort.** When I'm tired, fiction works better for me.
!!!

**对比表**

| Fiction books                         | Non-fiction books                  |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Strong sense of immersion             | Practical but somewhat utilitarian |
| Relieve stress & escape reality       | Feels like studying                |
| Doesn’t require much mental effort    | Demands greater focus              |
| Get emotionally involved in the story | Learn something useful             |
| Escape from reality                   | Information-focused                |
| Relax my mind                         | Requires more concentration        |

### 5.5 家人朋友的阅读偏好

**45秒压缩版**

!!! example
When it comes to reading habits, my family and friends have different preferences, often **related to their age and routines**（可以交代一下原因）.

My parents usually prefer non-fiction, especially biographies and history books. They like practical or informative content and enjoy reading carefully in the evening.

My friends, on the other hand, **are more into**(更喜欢) fiction—novels or fantasy stories. They enjoy **strong plots and vivid characters**, and many read during commutes（通勤）. For them, reading is more about entertainment.
!!!

### 5.6 阅读环境偏好

**提问**：Do you prefer reading in a quiet place or where there's background noise?

**45秒压缩版**

!!! example
I'd prefer reading in a quiet place.

When it's quiet, I can really get into the book, especially if the story is complex or emotional. **I don't have to keep rereading the same page**, and the whole experience feels smoother.

For example, when I read before bed, even **soft background noise can pull my attention away**. Silence helps me stay focused and **enjoy the story without feeling rushed**. So a quiet environment makes reading much more satisfying.
!!!
