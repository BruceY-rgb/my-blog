### reach out vs  catch up vs follow up vs sync up

- `to catch up`:to get updates from someone or about something
- `to reach out`:to begin with contact or communication
- `to follow up`:to continue communication after the first contact or activity
- `to sync up`:to arrange a meeting with someone so everyone understand
